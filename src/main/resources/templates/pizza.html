Cheese Pizza

Country of Origin: Italy  

INGREDIENTS

  • 3/4 cup pizza sauce
  • 8 ounces sliced mozzarella cheese
  • 1 and 1/2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 2–3 Tablespoons grated parmesan cheese
  • Ground basil or Italian seasoning, to taste

DIRECTIONS

  • Prepare the pizza dough, and preheat the oven to 475°F
  • Top the dough with the pizza sauce, then the sliced mozzarella, then the shredded mozzarella, grated Parmesan, then sprinkle with basil.
  • Bake for 12-15 minutes. For the last minute, move the oven rack to the top rack to really brown the edges.


Serves 1 (or 2 if you are cooking for a date)
